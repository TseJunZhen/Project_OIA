International Students Scholarship

    Taipei Tech Scholarships include "Foreign Student Scholarship" and "International Graduate Student Scholarship." The following offers a brief introduction to the two types of scholarships.
    Taiwan Scholarship is provided by the Taiwan Government. The purpose is to attract outstanding students to pursue academic degrees in Taiwan and to deepen their understanding of Taiwan’s academic environment and culture, thereby enhancing exchanges and building friendships between Taiwan and their home nations. Taiwan Scholarship is, in principle, granted to students from countries that have diplomatic relations with Taiwan. Special consideration, however, may also be given to students from other countries.
    Eligibility

    ·All current Taipei Tech international students who have completed at least one semester at Taipei Tech and fulfilled one of the criteria listed below are eligible to apply.

    ·Recipients of scholarship or subsidy offered by any government, research or educational institution will not be considered.



僑生(含港澳生)獎學金

    國立臺北科技大學為獎勵優秀僑生申請就讀本校，特提供「國立臺北科技大學僑生獎學金」。
    僑生於每年11月左右向國際事務處提出申請，需檢附獎學金申請表乙份、上一學年成績單、自傳及生活、經濟狀況說明以及其他有助審查之文件：如參加課外活動幹部證明，參加校內外競賽獲獎證明。

陸生獎學金
    新生：凡經由大學校院招收大陸地區學生聯合招生委員會申請通過，經本校錄取並註冊就讀之新生，擇優給獎，為期一年。
    在校生：具本校學籍之大陸地區研究生，可憑其學業成績、研究成果及指導教授推薦信，申請次年獎學金。
